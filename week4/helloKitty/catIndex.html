HELLO KITTY

About

Hello Kitty (full name Kitty White) is an iconic, mouthless, anthropomorphic character created by Sanrio in 1974.
Though designed as a British 8-year-old girl, she is a global phenomenon known as a "white cat with a red bow".
She lives in London, is five apples tall, loves baking cookies, and promotes friendship.

Likes

Hello Kitty loves baking cookies, eating her mama's homemade apple pie, and drinking tea.
Her hobbies include traveling, reading, playing the piano, and collecting cute items.
A friendly character, she loves making new friends, often saying, "you can never have too many friends".
